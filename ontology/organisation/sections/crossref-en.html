Scopec back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: http://w3id.org/devops-infra/organisation#Scope

This is a term commonly used to describe the reasons why a Resource or Resource Group (a Configuration Item) are handled or grouped together. It may be related to a geographical area, to some business context, may be part of some common infrastructure form the cloud provider, etc. Generally, a Scope is managed by a specific Department, which is responsible for it.

Tenantc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: http://w3id.org/devops-infra/organisation#Tenant

A tenant is usually understood as a container for items of an Organisation in a cloud, such as users, domains, subscriptions, etc. In other words, it identifies a group of users (e.g., from an organisation) who share a common access with specific privileges to some resources (configuration items) in the cloud. These resources may be provided by a single or by multiple providers (e.g., AWS, HWS, etc.) and belong to a specifc domain (e.g., normally named after a geographical region, as this is commonly used to provision physical resources from specific data centers).
